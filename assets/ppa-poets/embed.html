    About this network
    Poets are Wikidata-linked identities in the Princeton Prosody Archive. This is not a concordance of every co-mention. A line is drawn when two poets are repeatedly named near one another, within overlapping five-page windows, more often than their overall visibility in the archive would lead us to expect. The network therefore highlights recurrent pairings that stand out from background frequency. Colors mark communities of poets that tend to be grouped together. The publication-window slider keeps the same archive-wide layout but filters the view to poets and links active in a selected PPA publication window.
